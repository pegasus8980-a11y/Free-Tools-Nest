When should I use JPG instead of PNG?

JPG format is ideal for photographs and images where smaller file size matters more than transparency. JPG files can be 5-10x smaller than PNG, making them perfect for websites, emails, and social media posts.

Does quality matter when converting PNG to JPG?

Yes! JPG uses lossy compression. At 90% quality you get excellent visual quality with significantly smaller file size. Below 70% you may notice compression artifacts, especially in photos.
